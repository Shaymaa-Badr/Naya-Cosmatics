about us

Naya Cosmetics was found in 2022 in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (UAE). The founder & CEO, Mohammed Mumin is an entrepreneur with over 15 years of experience in the GCC (Golf Region) and Europe, He has extensive experience in the area of trade between the Netherlands and the UAE.

His background is into business development, marketing, management and sales of diversified products and services ranging from consumer electronics, personal care products, cosmetics, investments and banking solutions.

He holds the Master’s degree from the Open University in the Netherlands, specializing in management and AI (Artificial Intelligence) at work.

local & international presence

Naya Cosmetics has local & international partners, liaises with production facilities in the UAE, UK, the Netherlands, Morocco and Brazil.

Naya Cosmetics has strategic partnerships with major wholesalers, dealers & authorized resellers, agents, distributors, and cosmetics retailers around the world.

Naya Cosmetics has offices in the UAE and active trade & business relationships in, Iraq, the Netherlands, UK, Morocco, Brazil and Saudi Arabia.
