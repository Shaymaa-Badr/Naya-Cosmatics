about us

Naya Cosmetics was found in 2022 in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (UAE), to accommodate the high demand for beauty products and cosmetics in the region.

Naya Cosmetics facilitates the trade between beauty experts, dermatology specialists and manufacturers, key wholesalers and distributers in different countries throughout Asia, Europe, and the MENA region.

With its wide network of strategic partners, Naya Cosmetics continuously generates attractive revenues, creates value and provides its partners with key knowhow, operational and logistics support.

local & international presence

Naya Cosmetics has strong strategic alliances internationally. The company has active relationship with local production facilities in the UAE, UK, Morocco & the Netherlands.

Naya Cosmetics has a wide network of strategic relationships with cosmetics wholesalers, dealers, retail resellers, agents and key distributors around the world.

Naya Cosmetics has offices in the UAE and active trade & partnerships in other counties such as Iraq, Saudi Arabia and the Netherlands.
